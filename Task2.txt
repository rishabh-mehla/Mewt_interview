solution 1

assumptions 
*for each color(black blue red) there are 'n' balls each present in bag
*total sum of the balls(minimum) should be exactly 'm'

**code**

n=int(input('Enter Val'))
m=int(input('Enter Val'))
ans=0
red=min(n,(4*m)/3)
ans+=red
left=m-(red*0.75)
blue=min(n,2*left)
ans+=blue
left-=(blue*0.5)
black=min(n,4*left)
ans+=black
left-=(black*0.25)
if(left==0):
print(ans)
else:
print("solution not possible)
